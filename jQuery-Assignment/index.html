How to avoid lines

Get an early start

It's absolutely essential to get to the park when it opens and immediately get in line for all of the best rides you couldn't secure a fastpass for.

Hit lower priority rides later in the day

Afternoons can also be a great time to watch shows or parades. Lines tend to peak arounnd 4 or 5.
