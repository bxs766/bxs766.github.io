Fast 9

F9: The Fast Saga

In the 9th film in the Fast and Furious Saga (10th if you count Hobbs & Shaw), Dom (Vin Diesel) is adjusting to being a family guy, when he is suddenly confronted by his brother Jakob (John Cena), who is working with one of Dom's former flames, Cipher (Charlize Theron). Dom must begrudgingly leave retirement and reassemble his famed crew to take his brother down. Somewhere along the way, Han (Sung Kang), returns from the dead.

This film is expected to be the biggest of May, and likely the biggest worldwide grosser of the year. Audiences around the world have a deep affinity for all things Fast and Furious, so as long as the film delivers on its promise to be the Fastest and most Furious installment yet, it should be a huge success.
