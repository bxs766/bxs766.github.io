About Me

I am currently a sophomore at the University of Miami. I expect to graduate in May 2022 with a double major in Motion Pictures and Theatre Arts and a minor in Media Management. I hope to become a writer-director-actor in the film industry. During the summer after my freshman year of college, I co-wrote, co-directed, and starred in an original feature length film entitled "Rewrites". Additionally, when I was a senior in high school, I was nominated for the Paper Mill Playhouse Rising Star Award for Outstanding Lead Actor for my performance as Jean Valjean in my high school's production of Les Miserables. I have the experience, skillset, and knowledge base to succeed as a writer, actor, and director. I am also proficient in editing, cinematography, and sound. I will do any job I am asked to do, and will be happy to work as long as it is in the entertainment industry.
